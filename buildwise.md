System Workflow 

Users log in and access the dashboard. The admin creates projects and assigns managers. Managers add tasks, materials, and timelines. Workers update daily task progress. The system tracks expenses, materials, and overall progress. Documents are uploaded and shared. Notifications are sent for deadlines and updates. The dashboard shows project status with graphs and summaries.
